Shuhan ZHONG (钟书涵)

PhD Candidate, Computer Science and Engineering, HKUST

I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science and Engineering at the CSE Department in the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), under the supervision of Prof. Sheung-Han Gary Chan. I have two Master’s degree. I obtained my Master of Science degree (in Information Technology) from HKUST. Prior to that, I obtained my Master of Engineering degree (by research, in Geodesy and Surveying Engineering) from Wuhan University (WHU) under the supervision of Prof. Caijun Xu. I received my Bachelor’s degree (in Geodesy and Geomatics Engineering) from WHU.

My research focus on the analytics and modeling of sequential and time series data, for both scientific purposes and real-world applications.
